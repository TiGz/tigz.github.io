# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- Prospective consulting clients (CTOs, founders, heads of engineering) checking out Adam before or after an intro call.
- Consultancies/agencies evaluating Adam as a specialist delivery partner.
- Due-diligence readers (procurement, insurers, IR35 status reviewers) verifying that Adam's public footprint matches Higher Level Software Ltd's supplier posture.
- Occasional recruiters and old colleagues.

## Product Purpose

Adam Chesney's personal CV site (adamchesney.com / tigz.me). A single-page, print-friendly CV that presents Adam as CEO & Lead AI Engineer of Higher Level Software Ltd, an agentic AI consultancy, with 25 years of engineering history behind it. Success: a senior technical reader concludes within seconds that Adam builds production agentic systems and has the track record to back it; the post-2024 work reads as company engagements (supplier posture), not employment.

## Brand Personality

Senior, precise, quietly confident. The page should feel like it was engineered, not decorated: dark, code-native, exact. Personality is allowed here (this is the personal site; the future HLS company site stays strictly professional) but it lands in small touches, not loudness.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-slop landing pages: purple gradient meshes, glowing particles, gradient text.
- Matrix/hacker terminal cosplay: green-on-black, scanlines, fake window chrome, blinking-cursor theatrics everywhere.
- Freelancer-template portfolios: giant headshot hero, "Hi, I'm Adam 👋", skill progress bars.
- The 2023 SaaS scaffold: tracked uppercase eyebrow above every section, icon cards in identical grids.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — the page itself is evidence Adam ships polished, modern frontend; craft is the argument.
2. **Supplier, not employee** — post-Aug-2024 work is framed as Higher Level Software engagements; employment job titles end with the employment era.
3. **Typography does the work** — hierarchy through scale and weight on a disciplined grid; decoration only where it carries meaning (line-work diagrams over ornament).
4. **Print is a first-class citizen** — the CV must print/PDF cleanly (light, compact) from the same document.
5. **Specific beats superlative** — "10× throughput", "300M+ transactions/year", "first leisure GDS web service in the UK"; never "passionate innovator".

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA: body text ≥4.5:1 on the dark background, visible focus states, semantic landmarks and heading order, `prefers-reduced-motion` honoured (content never gated on animation).
